Wolf

A single wolf can consume 9kg (about 19.8lbs) of meat in one sitting! Once a wolf has found a mate, they usually stay together for life.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus
  • Average Length, Height, Weight: 41-63 inches in length, 31-33 inches in height, and about 88 pounds in weight
  • Average Lifespan: 6-8 years
  • Habitat: Forests, mountains, arctic tundra

Wolves are found in a variety of places and climates, including North America and the Arctic, almost all of Eurasia, and extending as far south as Saudi Arabia. Despite the highly social nature of wolves, single or mated pair wolves typcially have higher success rates in hunting than do large packs.
